Moviescope (student project at Hackbright)

Video Presentation



Moviescope is a personal movie journal, which helps users to track films they have watched or want to watch in the future. A user can search for a movie by title, add reviews and ratings, and store it in a personal journal. Also, Moviescope provides a list of recommendations matching the userâ€™s taste. A user can compile a wishlist of films and find watch options on streaming services and in theaters.

Technology Stack:
  • Backend: Python, Flask, PostgreSQL, SQLAlchemy
  • Frontend: JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, JASON, Jinja2, Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS3
  • APIs: OMDB, Recombee, JustWatch
  • Dataset: MovieLens
